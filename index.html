    Welcome

    A word from the Medical Director

    Welcome and thank you for visiting St. Daniel Comboni Hospital website. To our patients, visitors and other stakeholders, we aim to provide quality, holistic, patient centered health care services in a clean and safe environment. “I have come so that they may have life and have it in abundance” (John 10:10)

    This is the mission of Jesus Christ our master whose footsteps we follow in fulfilling his mission by serving all humanity. Our website is exceptionally interactive, collaborative and will help us to communicate and get feedback from our stakeholders in order to improve service delivery.

    Services

    The Services We Offer

    Out Patient

    Patients with health problems who visit the hospital for treatment, but might not require to be admitted for overnight care.

    General

    Patients admitted are critically ill and it includes the medical ward and surgical ward.

    Dental

    A variety of dental services for patients to prevent complications and maintain health of teeth.

    Peadiatrics

    A dedicated children’s ward with the aim of improving and maintainaing the health of infants.

    Imaging

    For the administration and provision of x-ray diagnostic and therapeutic services for patients.

    Operating Theatre

    A functional operating theatre working 24 hours to handle both major and minor surgical procedures.

    Anti-Retroviral

    The Hospital was accredited as a distribution Centre for Anti-Retroviral drugs by the Ministry of Health Uganda.

    Maternity Ward

    Provision of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Maternity Care, Neonatal Care, Elective and Emergency Cesarean section.

    Laboratory

    Provision of holistic quality, accurate, reliable and timely diagnostic medical laboratory services.

    Accident & Emmergency

    A well-established ambulance service system with well trained staff to handle emmergencies.

    Community Health Services

    A variety of routine and emergency medical services provided to support the community.
