Social Corprate Responsibility

The hospital has built houses for vulnerable patients, provided solar powered wheelchairs, it provides food to vulnerable patients within and outside the facility, it offers subsidized ambulance services especially for reproductive health care services, and the hospital has tried to bring services closer to its clients/ patients by putting up outreach sites in different areas.

Keep Comboni Clean

At St. Daniel Comboni Hospital Infection prevention control is our priority. The hospital has placed colour coded functional waste bins with bin liners in all wards and other service delivery points both indoor and outdoor. The colour codes are meant to guide the clients and staff on waste segregation.
We have an Infection Prevention control (IPC) team on ground to monitor and ensure maximum hygiene and cleanliness across the hospital.
